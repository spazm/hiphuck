The Infamous Lorax
Official Bid for Kaimana Klassik 23
Welcome to the web-based bid of The Infamous Lorax for Kaimana Klassik 23. We'd like to use this space to acquaint you with our team and what we're about.

This team is a solid cross-section of the New York City Ultimate scene, based around a core group of guys who love the game and its spirit. And although the team has no shortage of local talent, and is ready to compete, our primary goal is to play spirited, quality ultimate, and have a great time doing it. You'd be hard pressed to find a nicer bunch of folks who live in Brooklyn, Manhattan, and New Jersey.

We invite you to check out the "Bios" and "Philosophy" sections to find out more about who we are, where we're from, and what we're about.
